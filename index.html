DRACO MALFOY

Profile

Draco Malfoy is a key character in the Harry Potter series written by J.K. Rowling. He is first introduced as a student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and belongs to the Slytherin house, known for its ambition and cunning. Draco is characterized by his pale complexion, sleek blond hair, and piercing gray eyes. He comes from a wealthy and influential pure-blood wizarding family, the Malfoys, who hold deep beliefs in blood purity and maintain a strong connection to dark magic.

Throughout the series, Draco serves as a foil to the protagonist, Harry Potter. He is portrayed as arrogant, entitled, and quick to belittle those he deems beneath him. Draco often aligns himself with dark forces and holds prejudiced views towards Muggle-borns and those from less affluent backgrounds. He becomes a prominent rival to Harry, frequently engaging in confrontations and attempting to undermine him and his friends. Despite his negative traits, Draco's character also undergoes a significant transformation and displays moments of internal conflict and vulnerability.

As the story progresses, Draco's motivations and loyalties become more complex. While initially embracing his family's beliefs, Draco begins to experience the weight of the expectations placed upon him. He struggles with the pressure to conform to his father's desires while also grappling with his own doubts and fears. This internal conflict humanizes Draco and presents opportunities for growth and redemption. Ultimately, his character highlights the blurred lines between good and evil, showcasing the complexities and choices individuals face in a world filled with magic and darkness.

Activity

Kelas Herbologi (Herbology) is a subject taught at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. In this class, students learn about magical plants, their properties, and how to care for and cultivate them. Under the guidance of the herbology professor, students study various magical plants, ranging from the common to the exotic. They learn how to identify different plants, understand their magical properties, and acquire knowledge of their practical uses in potions, spells, and other magical applications.

In Herbology class, students get hands-on experience working in the Hogwarts greenhouses, tending to plants, pruning, repotting, and even dealing with more challenging and dangerous specimens. The class emphasizes the importance of patience, precision, and respect for nature. Through Herbology, students develop a deeper understanding of the natural world and how it intertwines with magic.

Kelas Ramuan (Potion) is one of the subjects taught at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. In this class, students learn the art of brewing magical potions using various ingredients and precise techniques. Under the guidance of the potions professor, students study complex recipes and instructions, understanding the interactions between different ingredients to create potions with desired effects. They learn to identify and gather magical plants, rare powders, and enchanted liquids.

Each class is spent carefully mixing and brewing potions, measuring each ingredient meticulously, and following strict instructions. The Potions class is known for being demanding, requiring precision and attention to detail, as even a small mistake in following instructions can result in undesired, or even dangerous, effects. However, for diligent students who master the art of potions, the class provides an opportunity to develop valuable skills and knowledge in the wizarding world, opening doors to creating healing potions, protective brews, and much more.
